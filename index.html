Hi, I'm Thiago Holleben, a PhD candidate at Dalhousie University. My advisor is Sara Faridi.


I'm mainly interested in combinatorics and its relations with commutative algebra, topology and geometry. During my MSc, I studied a particular case of the recent proof of the Rota-Heron-Welsh conjecture on the log-concavity of chromatic polynomials. My Master's thesis can be found here.
