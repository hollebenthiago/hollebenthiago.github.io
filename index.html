Hi, I'm Thiago Holleben, a PhD student at Dalhousie University.


I'm mainly interested in commutative algebra and its relations with combinatorics. During my MSc, I studied a particular case of the recent proof of the Rota-Heron-Welsh conjecture on the log-concavity of chromatic polynomials. My Master's thesis can be found here.

I'm also interested in scientific computing and mathematical modelling.
